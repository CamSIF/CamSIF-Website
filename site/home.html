Regular Meetings every Sunday

We are the only finance society in Cambridge with consistent, weekly sessions. Every Sunday, members gather in the Riley Auditorium in Clare College. Members receive a holistic insight into finance:

  • Dedicated training
  • Discussion into the latest trends
  • Opportunities to network with peers

Pitch Your Own Ideas

In the last two years, we pioneered sector divisions: dedicated teams of 6 led by an expereinced sector head. Divisions cover TMT; Consumer and Retail; Energy; Healthcare; Industrials; Real Estate; and Sustainability.

Over the year, each team develops industry-specific investment pitches which are presented at weekly meetings. Every year we send high-performing teams to the London Stock Pitch Competition where we achieved Top 4 in the UK last year.

Top-Tier External Events

We carefully prepare diverse events, from speakers and panels, to socials and competitions. Last year, we welcomed such figures as the Global Head of Research at Citi, Global Head of Financing Quants at UBS, and the Co-Head of Goldman Sachs EMEA ECM. For a full list of speakers, see our previous termcard.

We additionally collaborate to attend events such as the LSE Alternative Investments Conference. Through regular socials, members can network with likeminded peers and get to know the wider Cambridge community.

We provide eager students with a unique opportunity to gain experience in investment and markets.

No prior knowledge or experience in finance is required. We welcome Cambridge students of any degree course at any point in their education.
